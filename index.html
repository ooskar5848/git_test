An old japanese proverb

The japanese say we have three faces. The first face, we show to the world. The second face, we only show to close friends and family. And our third face is shown only to ourselves.

Which face do you show?

The first face is the least true representation of who we really are. The third face is the most representative of our true being. The second is somewhere in between. Do you show your first face constantly, even to the ones you are close with? Or maybe you show your second face to the world. Regardless, no one ever shows their third face.
